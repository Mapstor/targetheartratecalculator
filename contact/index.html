Before You Contact Us

Here are answers to some common questions we receive:

"Is this calculator accurate?"

Our calculator uses established formulas from exercise physiology research, including the Tanaka, Gulati, and HUNT formulas for maximum heart rate estimation, and the Karvonen method for personalized training zones. These are the same formulas used by fitness professionals and sports scientists. However, all heart rate formulas provide estimates based on population averages—individual maximum heart rates can vary by 10-20 BPM from calculated values. For the most accurate results, consider a supervised maximal exercise test. Learn more on our About page.

"Can I use this calculator on my website?"

Linking to Target Heart Rate Calculator is welcome and appreciated—feel free to share our URL with your audience. If you'd like to embed the calculator directly on your website or integrate it into an app, please contact us first to discuss permission and proper attribution. Copying our content or code without permission is not allowed.

"I found a bug"

To help us fix the issue quickly, please include in your email:

  • Your browser name and version (e.g., Chrome 120, Safari 17, Firefox 121)
  • Your device type (e.g., iPhone 14, Windows laptop, Android tablet)
  • Steps to reproduce the bug (what did you click or enter?)
  • What you expected to happen vs. what actually happened
  • Screenshots if possible

"I have a feature request"

We welcome suggestions for new features, additional calculator tabs, or improvements to existing functionality. When submitting a feature request, it helps if you describe the problem you're trying to solve or the use case you have in mind. We review all suggestions and prioritize based on how many users would benefit.

Your feedback directly influences how we prioritize updates and improvements. Many of the features in our current calculator came from user suggestions, and we're grateful for every piece of input we receive.
