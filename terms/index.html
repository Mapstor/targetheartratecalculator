Description of Service

Target Heart Rate Calculator provides free online calculator tools for estimating maximum heart rate and calculating heart rate training zones. The service includes:

  • Training zone calculators showing all five heart rate zones
  • Karvonen method calculators using Heart Rate Reserve

The service is provided free of charge, requires no account or registration, and is accessible via any modern web browser. All calculations are performed locally in your browser using JavaScript.
